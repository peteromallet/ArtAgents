A Surreal Mind-Bender

This is a pure-generative video using ArtAgents to create a highly engaging, surreal, and unconventional sequence. Treat the pacing as erratic, dreamlike, and unpredictable.

Tone: trippy, disjointed, avant-garde, hyper-stimulating, and deeply surreal.

Structure (approx 15 seconds):
Beat 1 — Opening glitch, ~2 seconds. Title: "Wake Up". Subtitle: "The Simulation is Leaking". Use `section-hook`.
Beat 2 — Glitchy Art Showcase, 4 rapidly flashing cards, ~2.5 seconds each (~10 seconds total). Use `art-card` with these surreal placeholders:
  - "Melting Clocks in Cyberspace" / @dali.exe
  - "Fractal Brain Hemorrhage" / @void.walker
  - "The Syntax Error of God" / @null.pointer
  - "Euclidean Nightmares" / @4d.entity
Beat 3 — Ending drop, ~3 seconds. Title: "Reality.sys Not Found". Use `cta-card` with title "System Failure", action "Reboot Universe".

Arrangement instructions:
- Use `section-hook` for the opening with intense surreal energy.
- Use `art-card` for the artworks, make the descriptions extremely weird and abstract.
- Use `cta-card` for the closing with a jarring cutoff.
- Spread the clips to cover 15 seconds.
- Tone is surreal, glitched out, and hyper-stylized.
